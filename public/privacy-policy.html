Privacy Policy

For the HydroShine Facebook app and booking system
Effective date: May 9, 2026
Last updated: May 9, 2026

HydroShine ("we", "us", or "our") operates a mobile car wash and auto detailing booking system and related communication tools, including Facebook Messenger lead handling, booking management, and customer follow-up.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and the choices available to customers and users.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as name, phone number, and address.
  • Booking details such as service type, requested date and time, notes, and estimated revenue.
  • Facebook-related identifiers such as Messenger thread IDs, lead form IDs, and page interaction metadata when a customer contacts us through Facebook products.
  • Communication history, including inquiries, follow-up messages, and booking confirmations.
  • Content submitted for service operations, including photos and videos related to a booking when provided by the customer or captured by HydroShine for service documentation.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and schedule services.
  • To confirm, manage, and complete bookings.
  • To track leads, repeat customers, and service history.
  • To improve customer service and maintain consistent communication.
  • To create internal operational records, content tracking, and business reporting.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the business, such as hosting, scheduling, communications, analytics, and future payment or CRM systems. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Facebook Data

When a customer contacts HydroShine through Facebook Messenger or a Facebook lead form, we may receive message content, page identifiers, thread identifiers, and booking-related details needed to respond to the inquiry and manage the service.

Data Retention

We retain booking and customer records for as long as needed for business operations, customer support, repeat service tracking, recordkeeping, and legal or tax purposes.

Your Choices

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting us.
  • You may stop contacting us through Facebook at any time.
  • You may opt out of non-essential follow-up by telling us directly.
