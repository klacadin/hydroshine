Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: May 9, 2026

If you want HydroShine to delete your information collected through Facebook or the booking system, send a request to:

Email: support@hydroshine.online
Subject: Data Deletion Request

What to include

  • Your full name.
  • Your phone number or the Facebook account name you used to contact us.
  • The booking date or approximate date if available.

What happens next

  • We will verify the request and locate the record.
  • We will delete or anonymize the information where required and reasonably possible.
  • We may retain information needed for legal, tax, or business recordkeeping purposes.
